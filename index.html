Research

Data-Driven Insights on Brain, Mind, and Behavior

My research merges Predictive Modeling & Data Insights with Sensory Data & Analysis, focusing on Brain, Mind, & Behavioral Neuroscience. Leveraging signals from eye-tracking, HRV, GSR, EEG, I explore cognitive load, emotional states, and micro-behaviors in real time.

Ultimately, I aim for learning-enabled, physics-informed architectures that uphold human values while harnessing AGI and advanced embedded system design to reshape computational neuropsychology. This approach enables early mental health detection, user-adaptive experiences, and a deeper understanding of human cognition in modern technology contexts.
